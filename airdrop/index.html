How it works

  1. 1. Load the leaderboard to pull live staking credit data from the Sui blockchain.
  2. 2. Select recipients from the leaderboard, paste addresses, or upload a wallet/amount CSV.
  3. 3. Choose the token and either enter one shared amount or let the CSV supply per-wallet amounts.
  4. 4. Click Execute — your wallet will ask you to confirm one or more batch transactions until the full airdrop is complete.
